### **V0.4 - Enhanced Curriculum Learning**
**Date:** December 11, 2025  
**Status:** ✅ COMPLETE, ANALYZED  
**Training Time:** ~70 minutes (17,500 episodes)

**Major Changes:**
- Extended training: 17,500 episodes (vs 10,000 in v0.3)
- Slower epsilon decay: 0.99995 (reaches 0.05 at ~6000 episodes)
- Task-based win condition: 4 pellets collected (not score-based)
- Decoupled episode termination from scoring
- Q-network logging system

**Curriculum:** [2500, 2500, 2500, 10000] = 17,500 episodes  
**Results:**
- Overall win rate: 37.79% (vs 2.74% in v0.3)
- Phase 1: 69.40% win rate ✅
- Phase 2: 64.60% win rate ✅
- Phase 3: 58.64% win rate ✅
- Phase 4: 17.98% win rate ⚠️ **PROBLEM IDENTIFIED**

**Key Discovery:**
Phase 4 plateau - no improvement over 10,000 episodes. Root cause: Hunter introduced too late and too strong (difficulty shock). This led to v0.5 redesign.

## 🔬 KEY INSIGHTS BY VERSION

### V0.1 → V0.3
**Lesson:** Curriculum learning is ESSENTIAL. Can't learn everything at once.

### V0.3 → V0.4
**Lesson:** Epsilon decay rate matters enormously. Slow and steady wins the race.

### V0.4 → V0.5
**Lesson:** Don't hide final challenge until the end. Introduce early at low difficulty, scale progressively. Build challenge-specific skills from day 1.
