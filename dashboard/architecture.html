Architecture: Gated Option Policies + Counterfactual HER

Versions Replay Phase 3 — v5 Research

Problem: Strategy Bimodality

After 6000 episodes of vanilla FSP training (v4), Krishna learned evasion but developed collection paralysis. It never approached pellets even when the Hunter was far away. The win condition (4 pellets) was reached only 6 times in 6000 episodes.

Root cause: The replay buffer contained only evasion-only transitions. The FSP pool evicted all weak early-game Hunters after ep 2000, leaving no safe practice time.

Solution

Two complementary mechanisms:
1. Gated Option Policies (GOP) — dual Q-heads (evade + collect) with a learned 3-input gate that blends them based on Hunter distance, pellets remaining, and lives.
2. Counterfactual HER (CHER) — post-episode relabeling that scans trajectories for "safe collect opportunities" Krishna missed and injects them with a +30 collection bonus.
