## 📋 Summary of Today's Bug Fixes

### **Bug #1: Epsilon Decay Too Fast** 🐛
- **Problem:** Epsilon hit 0.05 by episode 1,466 (should be ~0.86)
- **Cause:** Decaying every learning step (every 4 steps) instead of per episode
- **Fix:** Moved epsilon decay to end of episode in `main.py`
- **Files:** `agent/dq_agent.py`, `main.py`

### **Bug #2: Hunter Position Attribute** 🐛
- **Problem:** `AttributeError: 'AStarHunter' object has no attribute 'position'`
- **Cause:** Progressive Hunters use `self.pos`, environment expects `self.position`
- **Fix:** Added `@property` for compatibility
- **Files:** `environment/entities/hunter_progressive.py`

### **Improvement #1: Terminal UI** 🎨
- **Problem:** Unclear what columns mean, cramped spacing
- **Fix:** Two-line headers with labels, better spacing
- **Files:** `utils/terminal_display.py`
