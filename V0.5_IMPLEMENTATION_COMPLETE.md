## 🎯 WHAT'S DIFFERENT IN V0.5?

### **1. Progressive Hunter System (THE BIG ONE)**
**Before (v0.4):** No Hunter in Phases 1-3 → Suddenly A* Hunter in Phase 4  
**Now (v0.5):** Hunter from episode 1, difficulty increases progressively

| Phase | Hunter Type | Behavior | Difficulty |
|-------|-------------|----------|------------|
| 1 | Random | Wanders randomly | ★☆☆☆ Very Low |
| 2 | Greedy | Follows direct path | ★★☆☆ Low-Medium |
| 3 | Smart Greedy | Avoids walls, persistent | ★★★☆ Medium-High |
| 4 | A* | Optimal pathfinding | ★★★★ Expert |

### **2. Composite Success Scoring**
**Before:** Binary win/loss (0 or 1)  
**Now:** Multi-dimensional score (0.0 to 1.0) across 4 components

- Objectives (40%): Pellets collected
- Survival (30%): Lives preserved
- Efficiency (20%): Steps optimization
- Strategy (10%): Intelligent patterns

**Success Tiers:** S (Perfect), A (Excellent), B (Good), C (Acceptable), D (Poor), F (Failure)

### **3. Phase-Adaptive Epsilon Reset**
**Before:** Epsilon decays to 0.05 and stays there  
**Now:** Resets at phase transitions

- Phase 1 start: ε = 1.0 (full exploration)
- Phase 2 start: ε = 0.5 (balanced)
- Phase 3 start: ε = 0.3 (mostly exploit)
- Phase 4 start: ε = 0.2 (strategic exploration)

### **4. Reduced Catch Penalty**
**Before:** -20 penalty per catch  
**Now:** -10 penalty per catch

**Reasoning:** Encourages calculated risk-taking, agent learns "collect pellets while avoiding" not "just avoid"

### **5. Beautiful Terminal Output**
**Before:** Functional but basic text output  
**Now:** Color-coded phases, success tiers with emojis, progress bars, detailed summaries
